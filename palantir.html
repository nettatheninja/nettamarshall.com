Palantir

Palantir is a pretty secretive company. Hell, I’m almost afraid to disclosed I’ve worked there. I definitely can’t show (or even speak about) my main project from my time there, but I can show one of my first projects. Palantir does a lot of work with non-profits, all of which is provided at no cost to the organization. Every year, Palantir compiles a report highlighting some noteworthy achievements, and I had the honor of designing the 2014 report. I got to work with an illustrator that I really admire (Nick Slater), and we knocked out this project in about 2 weeks.
