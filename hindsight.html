Hindsight

Hindsight is a side project I launched in May of 2016. It’s one that I’m deeply passionate about and proud to put out into the world. In the past, I’ve mentored through Design Lab, and have taken on mentorship informally as well. After being a designer for over 13 years, I’m always trying to find ways to give back to the design community and share the knowledge and experience that I’ve accumulated. Whenever I get emails in my inbox from fresh creatives asking for advice, I often respond with things I would tell my younger self as I was just starting out. Hindsight is that same advice from many seasoned creatives.
